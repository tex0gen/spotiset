What is Spotiset?

Spotiset is a desktop application to help you find DJ/live set lists and add the tracks to your Spotify playlists.

How to use

On starting Spotiset, hit "login" and login to your Spotify account. Then search your favorite artists and DJs to find related sets. When you've found the set you want, you can either individually select tracks or make a playlist from all of them. Super simple!

Trouble installing Spotiset?

MacOS Installation

Because Spotiset is an unsigned application, you may have difficulties installing.

To get around this issue:

  • Open the .dmg file and drag the Spotiset application to your applications folder
  • In your Applications folder, double click to open Spotiset (A notice may appear about the program being unsigned)
  • Click the apple icon in the top left corner of your screen and click "System Preferences"
  • Click Security and Privacy
  • On the "general" tab, towards the bottom of the window you'll see a notice about Spotiset being unsigned. Click "open anyway".
  • When you open Spotiset, you may see a firewall notice. Click "allow" or you will not be able to get listings for tracklists.

Windows Installation

  • Extract the .zip file
  • Open Spotiset.exe
